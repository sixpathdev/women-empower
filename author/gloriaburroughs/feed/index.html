]]> Graduate Gala Celebration

Strong, capable, spirited, and diligent are just a few words we use to describe our graduates who have overcome adversity and hardships. Women who were once experiencing homelessness have now gone on to find stable jobs, safe housing, and receive the care they need, and we think that’s incredible. Each day, we are inspired by their ever-growing accomplishments and willingness to achieve even more.

We had the chance to celebrate the accomplishments of our hard-working graduates at our grad gala. On this dazzling night, our graduates had a blast as they dressed up and enjoyed a live DJ, a delicious catered meal, a fun photo booth, raffle prizes, and more. Take a look at some of the fun below!
