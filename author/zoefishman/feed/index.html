YOU CAN MAKE YOUR BDOG GIFT TODAY
TOGETHER, WE EMPOWER!
www.bigdayofgiving.org/womens-empowerment

Big Day of Giving (BDOG) is an annual online giving movement that unites the region’s nonprofit sector to help raise funds for the organizations that strengthen the capital area. For BDOG this year, we have a goal of raising $100,000 to help ensure WE can continue to empower.

For the past 20 years, the Women’s Empowerment community has supported over 1,700 women and their 3,700 children in graduating from our programs. Together, we have accomplished a lot and we have come a long way, but our work is not done. Now, women face new challenges due to the pandemic and associated economic and housing crises. We need you to continue to walk alongside these resilient women.

MAKE YOUR GIFT NOW FOR BDOG ON MAY 6TH!

MAKE A DONATION TODAY!
By making your early BDOG donation you are helping to ensure that we can continue to create pathways for women and children to rise from homelessness. Guess what!?! Poswall Family Foundation is making it possible for you to double your impact by matching your gifts, dollar for dollar, up to $50,000.  

ATTEND!
Community empowers! All are welcome to join us on May 6, Big Day of Giving, anytime between 11 a.m.-1 p.m. to write encouraging notes to be given to future students and grads of our programs! In partnership with the National Council of Jewish Women Sacramento, socially distanced note-writing stations, supplies, and sample notes will be set up! See you there! 

CAMPAIGN FOR WE!
Take your generosity a step further by setting up personal fundraising page and rallying your personal community around this important work!
Creating a fundraiser is as easy as 1,2,3…
1. Visit our BDOG PAGE and at the top select “Fundraise.”
2. Add your fundraiser information. Here is a TOOLKIT for sample text and more.
3. Ask your friends and family to support!

To make a BDOG donation you can:
1. Mail checks made out to Women’s Empowerment (with BDOG in the memo line) to 1590 North A Street, Sacramento, CA 95811
2. Click the button below, to give online through the BDOG portal! 

Make a Gift To Empower

New Elk Grove Affordable Apartment Complex Opens Lottery. You can apply now!

Attention Women’s Empowerment students and Graduates, there is a new low-income housing development that is currently underway and set to be completed in April of this year. Right now, the city of Elk Grove has opened up a “lottery” system that will close on March 3, 2021. This lottery is not a guarantee that you will get an apartment; however, it puts you in for a chance to potential be one of the persons and families that receive a low-income unit.

The drawing for units will be on March 5. The eligibility for the lottery is income-based and capped at those earning up to 60% of the median area income. So that means that you can not make more than $36,000 a month.
You can Apply Now HERE. 
If you have any questions or need support with your application please reach out to WE’s Housing Specialist, Kaitlyn, at kaitlyn@womens-empowerment.org.
