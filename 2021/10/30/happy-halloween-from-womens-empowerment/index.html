Happy Halloween from Women’s Empowerment

We’re getting into the Halloween Spirit

We had some spooky Halloween fun this Friday at Women’s Empowerment. Our students, staff, and children in the CDC got into the Halloween spirit this year by dressing up in some fun costumes. To kick off the Halloween weekend, the children in the CDC went from office to office and trick or treated with the staff.

Special thanks to Donna Valentine for bringing pumpkin muffins for a fun fall-inspired Fri-YAY snack for our students. A perfect way to end this haunted weekend.

WOMEN’S EMPOWERMENT

The mission of Women’s Empowerment is to educate and empower women who are experiencing homelessness with the skills and confidence necessary to get a job, maintain a healthy lifestyle, and regain a home for themselves and their children.
